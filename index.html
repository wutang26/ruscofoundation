    About Rusco Foundation

    Promoting resilience for accident and disaster victims.

    Supporting Recovery, Restoring Stability, Rebuilding Lives

    Rusco Foundation is a humanitarian organization committed to protecting and restoring the economic well-being of individuals and families affected by accidents and disasters. We believe that recovery is not only about survival, but about rebuilding a stable future.

    We collaborate with social security institutions such as the National Social Security Fund (NSSF) to ensure effective service delivery for people working in both government and non-governmental organizations. Our goal is to help individuals understand and access social protection systems that secure their future.

    Benefits of Social Security (NSSF)
    • Funeral Assistance Benefit
    • Maternity Benefits
    • Medical Care Services
    • Unemployment Benefits
    • Old Age Pension
    • Disability Pension
    • Survivor’s Pension

    We stand with victims in their most difficult moments and walk with them toward recovery.

    • Long-term economic recovery and livelihood support
    • Community-based programs that strengthen resilience and stability
    • Education on how to join social security funds such as NSSF
    • Guidance on financial protection and risk management solutions

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Answers about our support for accident and disaster victims

    Who can receive help from Rusco Foundation?

    We support individuals and families affected by accidents and disasters who are facing economic hardship and need assistance to recover and rebuild.

    What type of assistance do you provide?

    Our support includes emergency financial aid, livelihood recovery programs, community rebuilding initiatives, and guidance for long-term economic stability.

    How can someone apply for assistance?

    Victims or their representatives can contact us through our support channels or local partners. Applications are reviewed based on urgency and need.

    How can I support Rusco Foundation?

    You can support our mission through donations, volunteering, partnerships, or advocacy that helps expand assistance to victims.
