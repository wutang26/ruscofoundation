## 🌍 About Rusco Foundation

    Rusco Foundation is dedicated to **building economic security for accident and disaster victims**. Our mission is to provide support, resources, and guidance to help individuals and families recover and thrive after unexpected events.  

    With our headquarters in **Kirumba, Mwanza, Ilemela**, and a branch in **Moshi (P.O. Box 1910)**, we are committed to reaching communities across Tanzania and creating sustainable solutions that make a real difference.  

    At Rusco Foundation, we believe in **empowering people, fostering resilience, and ensuring that no one faces hardship alone**.
