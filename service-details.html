Our Services

Rusco Foundation provides structured economic recovery programs that help accident and disaster victims rebuild their lives.

Emergency Assistance Livelihood Recovery Community Projects Victim Advocacy Training Programs

Immediate Help When It Matters Most

We respond rapidly to accidents and disasters by providing financial and social assistance that stabilizes victims and their families.

Emergency Financial Assistance Program

Our emergency support program ensures that victims of accidents and disasters receive immediate financial aid to cover urgent needs such as shelter, medical care, and essential living expenses.

  • Rapid response financial relief
  • Family stabilization support
  • Recovery planning assistance

Beyond emergency aid, Rusco Foundation works closely with survivors to design long-term recovery strategies that restore economic independence.

Through partnerships and community engagement, we build sustainable recovery systems that protect families from falling back into crisis.
